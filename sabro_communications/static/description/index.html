Business Workflow Integrated Communications Platform

Transform Odoo into Business Phone System

Product Description

A simple, easy to use tool to enable communications to your business. SaBRO Communications integrate Odoo contacts and users to SaBRO platform in order to enable calling features on odoo platform. SaBRO Communications provides all necessary standard and advanced features to enable telephony to your business.

Standard Features

Incoming Calls

Get your business calls on your odoo without external tools and setup overhead.

Outgoing Calls

Make local, national and international calls using webRTC from Odoo.

Voicemail

Attend to your missed calls and customer queries even if you are unavailable.

Music On Hold

Keep your callers' entertained while forwarding or transferring their call to external number or agents.

Call forwarding

Forward business calls instantly to reduce the waiting time of your callers.

Call History

Track and monitor all your business calls to ensure you do not miss any potential leads.

Advance Features

Business Numbers

Add multiple phone numbers from more than 80+ countries and extensions to receive calls from anywhere, anytime.

Number Routing

Map your business numbers to the user extension, department ring group or call flow to connect your customer.

Default Call Flow

Take benefit of default call routing and divert a plan to assure each call gets attended even in case of disruption.

Business Hours Call Handling

Create a custom call flow to efficaciously route or divert calls during office hours for different timezone and different working days.

Closed Hours Call Handling

Create a custom call flow and handle the calls gracefully coming in during out-of-office hours or holidays.

Effective call Routing with Ext & IVR

You can create a custom extension and / or an IVR menu to effectively route and handle the incoming calls.

Custom Greeting Message

Personalize the call experience by greeting your customers with a welcome message.

Real Time Call Recording

Listen and analyse your customer conversation to understand the requirements of your callers.

Live call transfer

Transfer calls without disconnecting ongoing calls to enhance your callers’ call experience.

Direct & Group SMS

Send and receive SMS on user's account and assigned to the department to manage the incoming SMS effectively.

Direct & Group FAX

Send and receive business Fax from SaBRO. Assign the Fax to the user or department to respond to the sender efficiently.

Live Dashboard

Monitor the number of calls connected and missed by each department in your organization.
